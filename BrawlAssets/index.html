Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I find renders?

There are couple of renders channel where assets providers and graphics designers will post renders and assets. However, you can find the Brawl Assets graphics pack which includes all renders here.

How do I earn points?

All you need to do is be active in the chat and you will start getting your Points. You can type p!points to see your points.

Can someone make me a thumbnail?

Unfortunately, we don't make free thumbnails for members. However, we believe that with the YouTube tutorials here and the renders here and some pre-made thumbnails here, you can make your own OP thumbnails yourself!

What are the rewards of the points?

You can earn exclusive custom roles, custom renders, logos, banners, etc! Please refer to the full list of the rewards here.

How to get roles within the server?

You can get the basic self roles here. Simply click on the reaction and if the number turns blue, you have successfully got a role!

I need an asset for my design. Where should I ask?

It's your lucky day man. You can request any assets in here and members or designers will get back to you ASAP! You can also ask for help in #🤝┃design-help.

How can I advertise my own YouTube videos or other social media?

There are 7 different ways to advertise in this server! Watch out!

  • 1. Become partner with this server #🤝┃partners
  • 2. Boost the server and gets shoutouted #👏┃serverbooster-advertising
  • 3. Make a tutorial video and get featured in #📺┃youtube-tutorials
  • 4. Become the holy ones and post in #📺┃yt-creators
  • 5. Become a designer and post in #📬┃designer-contacts
  • 6. Sponsor a giveaway #🎉┃giveaways
